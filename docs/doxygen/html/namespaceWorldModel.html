struct  CShape
 The CShape struct is the basic definition of a shape in the world model. In this case its a gear, kit or vessel. Kits and vessels have slots that hold the gears. The slots are defined for holders offset from the centroid of the holder. Each shape has a unique name, a type defined as a small, medium or large gear and vessel holders, and kits that hold combinations of gear types. More...
